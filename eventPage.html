The biggest party of the year is back! Henderson Bros. Burlesque has been rocking audiences since the first installment in 2013, and now the show returns to the Thigpen Main Stage. This year we're excited to feature guest performer Zatanna Zor-Elle from the windy streets of Chicago, IL. You'll also enjoy a fresh show of new bawdy acts, songs, and teases from your favorite HBB performers. This show will be the last for a spell as HBB takes as respite in the coming seasons. Don't miss Columbia's definitive Burlesque experience!
